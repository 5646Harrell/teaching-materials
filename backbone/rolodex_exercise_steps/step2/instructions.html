Exercise 3 - Making a View

In this exercise, we will be creating a View. We will be instantiating it with our previous model.

Instructions

  1. Open SpecRunner.html in a browser
  2. Click on Spec List on the upper-right hand corner
  3. Click on Exercise 3
  4. Make changes in src/app.js to make all the tests pass.
  5. In index.html, add a <script> tag right before the closing </body> tag. Inside, use jQuery to listen for $(document).ready(). In the callback, render and append your View to the $('body')

Bonus - Do something fancy

  1. Do something fancy with that onClick event, and maybe add some more events on top of that!
